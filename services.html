K&G Counseling Services

Mental Health and Substance Abuse Counseling

  • Address any trauma or past experiences that may impact mental health
  • Form coping mechanisms for dealing with stress & negative emotions
  • Identify triggers that may lead to substance abuse
  • Most insurances accepted
  • Self-Pay $80 an hour

Collaborative Couples Counseling

  • Develop a shared vision for the future of the relationship
  • Learn how to navigate life transitions and changes together
  • Establish strategies to improve emotional intimacy
  • Insurance not accepted
  • Self-Pay $150 an hour

Conflict Resolution

  • Individual meeting with business owner/manager to discuss needs for employees
  • Minimum of 2-hour session required for booking 
  • Please contact us directly for pricing
  • Deposit of 1/2 agreed upon price due at time of contract to secure date on calendar

Anger Management Counseling

  • Build coping methods for dealing with anger in a healthy way
  • Create a support system to maintain motivation and accountability
  • Understand how to communicate confidently and effectively 
  • Most insurances accepted
  • Self-Pay $80 an hour

Case Management

  • Help clients identify and access resources, such as housing, healthcare & financial assistance.
  • Form a plan for achieving specific goals
  • Coordinate care with other healthcare providers and community organizations
  • Available to clients with Kentucky Medicaid
