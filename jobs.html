Jobs

Therapist

Seeking a fully licensed mental health therapist (LPCC or LCSW) with a minimum of 1 year post licensure experience. Preferred substance abuse experience in addition to mental health experience. Clinician must be self-motivated, have strong attention to detail, and be able to work as a team player. Clinician must be able to maintain requirements for their licensure on their own. An ideal candidate is up to date with research-based treatment modalities.

Targeted Case Manager

The Case Manager’s primary job function is to enhance the overall treatment process by assisting counselors in meeting the overall treatment needs of the clients. This position requires an understanding of mental health and substance use disorders in order to address barriers, better tailor health messages, provide links to community resources and better identify a network of potential referral sources. The Case Manager will help the patient access support across multiple systems and frameworks of care.

Qualifications:

  • Bachelor’s degree or Master’s degree in social work, psychology, or other human services field from an accredited institution or be a certified alcohol and drug counselor who has a bachelor of arts/science degree depending on the state requirements.
  • Have at least one (1) year of full-time employment experience working directly with adults in a human service setting after completing the educational requirements.
  • Ability to work with a diverse population.
  • Understanding of HIPAA, Federal, State standards & regulations.
  • Self-starter, able to work autonomously.
  • Customer service focused, eager and energetic.

Please send inquires or resumes to katy@kandgcounseling.com.
