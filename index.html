As long as there is breath in your lungs, it's never too late to claim the life you've always dreamed of.

Katy Whitley

&

Gina Zanon

Katy and Gina met while working at another agency and became fast friends. Over time they realized they wanted to hone in on their niches and branch out on their own, and so K & G was born! We are excited to be part of the women-owned businesses in Bowling Green and continue to serve our community.

 

We currently offer mental health, substance abuse, and anger management services to individuals and couples 15 years of age and older. We also offer conflict resolution consultations for businesses whose employees are struggling to get along. 

We are located in Bowling Green, Kentucky and offer both in-person and telehealth appointments.

 

Together we have over 15 years experience in the counseling field and enjoy providing our clients with quality service, making sure to stay updated and integrated with trainings and the newest therapies.
